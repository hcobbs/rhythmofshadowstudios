Privacy Policy

Rhythm of Shadow Studios, LLC — Effective April 1, 2026

We don't collect your data. We don't want it. We don't store it, sell it, share it, or look at it. Our apps run on your device, stay on your device, and mind their own business. So should everyone else.

That's the whole policy. Everything below is the legal scaffolding that says the same thing in ways that hold up in court.

1. Who We Are

Rhythm of Shadow Studios, LLC is a single-member limited liability company organized under the laws of the State of Missouri, United States.

2. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications, games, and software published by Rhythm of Shadow Studios, LLC ("our apps," "our software," "the app") and to our website at rhythmofshadowstudios.com.

3. Data Collection

We collect no personal data. None.

Specifically, we do not collect, process, store, transmit, sell, share, or otherwise handle:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any identifying information
  • Device identifiers, advertising identifiers (IDFA), or device fingerprints
  • Location data of any kind
  • Usage analytics, session data, event tracking, or behavioral data
  • Crash reports linked to any user or device identifier
  • Payment information
  • Photos, contacts, microphone input, camera input, or any other device resource beyond what is required for core gameplay as disclosed at the time of installation
  • IP addresses
  • Cookies or cross-site tracking identifiers

Our apps do not require you to create an account, log in, register, or provide any information of any kind to use them.

4. Data Storage

All app data — including game saves, settings, and preferences — is stored locally on your device only. We have no servers. We have no databases. We have no backend that receives anything from your device related to your use of our apps.

We do not use iCloud, Google Drive, or any cloud sync service to store or transmit your gameplay data without your explicit, affirmative action to enable such a feature, where applicable.

5. Third-Party Services

Our apps do not integrate advertising networks, analytics platforms, data brokers, or social media SDKs.

To the extent any third-party frameworks are used (such as Apple's native iOS frameworks), those frameworks operate under Apple's own privacy policies and terms. We do not independently transmit data to Apple or any other third party beyond what occurs as a standard function of the iOS operating system itself, which is outside our control and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

We do not use third-party crash reporting services that link crash data to user or device identifiers.

6. Multiplayer and Group Features

Where our apps include multiplayer or group play features, those features operate exclusively on a single physical device shared among players in the same physical location. No data is transmitted over a network. No player accounts are created. No user information is exchanged with any server or third party.

7. Advertising and Purchases

Our apps contain no advertisements of any kind. Our apps contain no in-app purchases, microtransactions, subscriptions, or premium content gating of any kind.

8. Children's Privacy

Our apps do not collect personal information from any user, including children under the age of 13. Because we collect no data whatsoever, our apps are compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) by design.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, contact us immediately at the address in Section 1 and we will take immediate action — though given that we collect nothing, this scenario is effectively impossible by our architecture.

9. Your Rights

Because we collect no personal data, there is nothing for us to access, correct, delete, port, or restrict on your behalf. Nevertheless, we acknowledge the following rights under applicable law:

  • California Residents (CCPA/CPRA): You have the right to know what personal information is collected, to delete personal information, and to opt out of the sale of personal information. We collect no personal information and sell nothing. Your rights are fully satisfied by default.
  • EU/EEA and UK Residents (GDPR/UK GDPR): You have rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection. We hold no personal data on you. There is nothing to act on. Your rights are satisfied by our architecture.
  • All Users: If you have any questions or concerns, contact us at the address in Section 1. Because we hold no data on any user, any response to a rights request is inherently immediate — there is nothing to retrieve, correct, or delete.

10. Data Security

We protect your data by not having it. The most secure data is data that was never collected in the first place. Our architecture enforces this by design, not by policy alone.

11. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the updated version at rhythmofshadowstudios.com/privacy with a revised effective date. Material changes will also be noted in app update release notes. Continued use of our apps following any update constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

We do not anticipate material changes, because "we collect nothing" is a stable position.
